About Us

Welcome to $ami hotel, where luxury meets comfort. Our hotel is dedicated to providing you with an exceptional stay, whether you're here for business or leisure. Situated in the heart of addis Ababa, we offer a convenient and stylish retreat for travelers from around the world.
